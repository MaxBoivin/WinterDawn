If a [lexicWord]#character# wants to attempt a task they're quite skilled at, a [lexicWord]#skill test# might not be required.
If his allowed [lexicWord]#dice-pool# contains a sufficient number of dice and if he is not under stress, the task should simply be considered as successful.

The required amount of dice in the [lexicWord]#dice-pool# per [lexicWord]#difficulty level# per type of dice are the following:

.Requirement for Automatic Success
[cols="7*^.^",options="header", caption ="", width = 50%, align="center"]
|===
| Difficulty Level | d4 | d6 | d8 | d10 | d12 | d20
| 1 | 6 | 3 | 2 | 2 | 2 | 1
| 2 | 11 | 5 | 4 | 3 | 3 | 2
| 3 | 16 | 8 | 6 | 4 | 4 | 2
| 4 | 21 | 10 | 7 | 5 | 5 | 3
| 5 | 26 | 12 | 9 | 7 | 6 | 3
| 6 | 31 | 15 | 10 | 8 | 6 | 4
| 7 | 35 | 17 | 12 | 9 | 7 | 5
| 8 | 40 | 19 | 13 | 10 | 8 | 5
| 9 | 44 | 21 | 15 | 11 | 9 | 6
|===

The threshold for automatic success is if the probability are over 80%.
