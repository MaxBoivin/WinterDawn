Physical feats are not only influence by the [lexicWord]#strength# of a [lexicWord]#character# but also by his [lexicWord]#athleticism skills#.
While [lexicWord]#strength# is the raw power, [lexicWord]#athleticism skills# represent the technical ability that allows to multiply the effectiveness of the [lexicWord]#attribute#.
