What is WinterDawn RPG?

A dice system with a strong tactile feedback. You truly feel the difference if you roll for something you're good at or something you're not.

A classless system. Your skills define what you are.

A level-less system. You skills improve as you use them.

No useless skills; each one has specific mechanical advantage.

A character creation based on the character background.

Six different types of magic, each giving access to a different array of spells and coming with their own particular risks.

Different types of weapons and armours with considerations other than aesthetic.

A tactical combat system based on choices and trade-offs.

A damage system where injuries have consequences.

Game mechanics representing the spirit of the characters. Adventuring can take a toll on your character's morale and affect their performance.

Game mechanics representing the personality of the characters and affecting the chance of success for certain tasks.

WinterDawn RPG is currently in pre-alpha. We will soon enter the alpha phase and begin open alpha-test. If you would like to participate in play test, send us a message at PlayTest@WinterDawnRPG.com.
